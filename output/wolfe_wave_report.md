## What is a Wolfe Wave?

A Wolfe Wave is a **natural 5-wave reversal pattern** discovered by Bill Wolfe. It identifies points of equilibrium in price action where supply and demand converge.

**Bullish Wolfe Wave (Buy Setup):**
```
  P2 ----__                  EPA Target
  /        \    P4 ----__   /
 /          \  /        \ /
P1           P3          P5  <-- Entry (Sweet Zone)
```
- P1-P3-P5 form the lower trendline (support)
- P2-P4 form the upper trendline (resistance)
- Lines CONVERGE (wedge pattern)
- P5 touches/overshoots the 1-3 line = **Sweet Zone** = Entry
- Target (EPA) = 1-4 line extended to P5 time

**Bearish Wolfe Wave (Sell Setup):** Mirror image of bullish.
